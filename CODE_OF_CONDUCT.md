# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and productive environment for all contributors.

This project focuses on technical collaboration around:
- system building
- cross-compilation
- containerized environments
- reproducible infrastructure

---

## Expected behavior

Participants are expected to:

- Be respectful and constructive
- Focus on technical discussion
- Accept feedback professionally
- Help maintain a clean and understandable codebase

---

## Unacceptable behavior

The following behaviors are not acceptable:

- Harassment or discrimination of any kind
- Offensive or toxic language
- Personal attacks or insults
- Deliberate sabotage of builds or workflows
- Ignoring established contribution guidelines

---

## Technical disagreements

Disagreements are normal in system-level projects.

When they occur:

- Focus on facts and technical reasoning
- Provide reproducible examples
- Avoid emotional or personal framing
- Prefer documentation-backed arguments

---

## Project philosophy

This project values:

- Simplicity over complexity
- Reproducibility over convenience hacks
- Transparency over hidden behavior
- Maintainability over premature optimization

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may:

- Request changes to contributions
- Reject non-compliant pull requests
- Remove disruptive contributions
- Ban contributors in extreme cases

---

## Contact

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please open a private issue or contact the maintainers directly.

We aim to resolve issues fairly and constructively.
